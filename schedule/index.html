SUBSCRIBE TO OUR CALENDAR

Never miss a practice, open gym, or tournament. Add our calendar to your device and get automatic updates whenever we add or change events.

01

Google Calendar

Click the "+ Subscribe" button above, or paste this calendar URL into Google Calendar under "Other calendars" → "From URL":

https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/elevatelawrencevb@gmail.com/public/basic.ics
02

Apple Calendar (iPhone/Mac)

Open Settings → Calendar → Accounts → Add Account → Other → Add Subscribed Calendar, then paste the iCal URL shown in step 1.

03

Outlook

In Outlook, go to Calendar → Add Calendar → From Internet, then paste the iCal URL. Events sync automatically.
