PASSING & SERVING CLINIC

MONDAY, MAY 11  ·  6:00–7:30 PM

One focused session on the two skills that decide every rally — receiving the serve and starting the point. For current 7th & 8th graders heading into 8th and 9th grade.

One 90-minute clinic with two skill blocks. We isolate the platform-and-angle system on the passing side and rebuild a repeatable serving routine on the other — then tie them together against live reps. Small group, high contact count, lots of ball control.

WHAT WE'LL WORK ON

  • Passing — platform construction, angle to target, movement & float reads
  • Serving — toss consistency, contact point, target zones, in-game routine
  • Live application — graded serve / serve-receive against the team

WHO IT'S FOR

Players currently in 7th or 8th grade (going into 8th or 9th grade in fall 2026). Bring whatever you've got — first-year players are welcome alongside club kids.
