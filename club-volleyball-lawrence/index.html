Parent's Guide

CLUB VOLLEYBALL IN LAWRENCE, KS

Choosing a junior club is a big decision. Here's how to compare programs in the Lawrence area — on coaching, team size, cost, and travel — and find the right fit for your athlete.

If your athlete is ready to move beyond rec or school ball, club volleyball is the next step — more coaching, more reps, and more competition. Lawrence families have a few good options, and the "best" club is the one that fits your athlete's goals, your budget, and how far you're able to travel. Below is what to look for, the programs in the area, and how to try out.

Comparing Programs

WHAT TO LOOK FOR IN A CLUB

Six things worth asking every program before you commit.

🏐

Coaching Philosophy

Is the environment positive and development-focused, or win-at-all-costs? Ask how coaches handle mistakes and playing time.

👥

Team Size & Playing Time

Smaller rosters usually mean more reps and more court time. Ask how many athletes are on each team.

💵

Cost & Fees

Ask what the season fee includes — coaching, gym time, tournament entries, uniforms — and what's extra.

🚗

Travel & Tournaments

How many tournaments, and how far? Travel load is one of the biggest differences between programs.

📅

Age Groups & Fit

Make sure the club fields your athlete's age group. Use the HOA/USAV age calculator if you're unsure.

📍

Practice Location

Where and how often are practices? A local gym in Lawrence saves a lot of weeknight driving.

Common Questions

CLUB VOLLEYBALL FAQ

What club volleyball options are there in Lawrence, KS?

Families in the Lawrence area typically compare a few junior programs, including the Lawrence Landsharks, Starlings, and Elevate Lawrence Volleyball Club. Each has its own age groups, fees, and tournament schedule, so it's worth contacting each club directly to find the best fit for your athlete.

How much does club volleyball cost?

Fees vary widely by program, number of tournaments, and how much travel is involved. When comparing programs, ask what the season fee includes — coaching, gym time, tournament entries, uniforms — and whether there are additional travel or equipment costs.

When are club volleyball tryouts in Lawrence?

Junior club tryouts in the Lawrence area are generally held in the summer for the following season. Elevate holds 12U and 15U tryouts at Holcom Park Recreation Center — see our tryouts page for current dates and registration.

What age groups does Elevate offer?

Elevate currently fields 12U and 15U teams, practicing locally in Lawrence. Not sure which age group your athlete falls in? Use the HOA/USAV age calculator to check the correct division.
