TRYOUT FAQ

What if my child has never played club?

No problem at all. We evaluate potential and attitude, not just existing skill. We're building these teams from the ground up and want athletes who are eager to grow.

Will every athlete make a team?

We're filling a 12U and 15U roster, so spots are limited. Athletes who aren't placed will receive honest feedback and are welcome at our upcoming open gyms.

When are tryouts?

We evaluate players on a rolling basis throughout the season. Fill out the tryout form above and we'll reach out to schedule a time that works — for both 12U and 15U.

What does the season commitment look like?

Typically 2 practices per week plus weekend tournaments. The full season schedule will be shared with team families through our team app once rosters are set.
