TRYOUT FAQ

What if my child has never played club?

No problem at all. We evaluate potential and attitude, not just existing skill. We're building these teams from the ground up and want athletes who are eager to grow.

Will every athlete make a team?

We're filling a 12U and 15U roster, so spots are limited. Athletes who aren't placed will receive honest feedback and are welcome at our open gym sessions.

There's only one tryout date — what if we can't make it?

Contact us as soon as possible. We may be able to arrange an individual evaluation depending on timing and roster availability.

Do we need to stay for both sessions?

No — players only attend the session for their age group. 12U athletes come for Session 1, 15U athletes come for Session 2. Arrive 15 minutes early for your session's check-in.

What does the season commitment look like?

Typically 2 practices per week plus weekend tournaments. The full season schedule will be shared with team families through our team app once rosters are set.
